Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

MovoraStar ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at movorastar.live (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using the Website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Website.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

MovoraStar does not require user registration, account creation, or the submission of personal information to use our service. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Home or business addresses
  • Payment or financial information
  • Government-issued identification
  • Usernames or passwords
  • Biometric or genetic data

3. Information We May Collect Automatically

When you visit our Website, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically through third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics). This may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Website
  • Referring URL or exit page
  • General geographic location (country or city level only)
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address (anonymized where possible)

This information is aggregated and anonymized. It cannot be used to identify you personally and is used solely to improve the performance and user experience of the Website.

4. Local Storage & Cookies

We use client-side LocalStorage and similar browser storage mechanisms to maintain user experience preferences. Specifically, we may store:

  • Language preference selections
  • Legal disclaimer acceptance status
  • Preferred streaming source settings
  • Auto-play toggle preferences

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You may clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.

5. Third-Party Embedded Content

Our Website allows users to view media content that is embedded from third-party streaming providers. When you access such embedded content, the third-party provider may set its own cookies, collect data, or track your activity. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

MovoraStar does not control, monitor, or assume any responsibility for the data collection practices of these third-party providers. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services accessed through our Website.

6. Third-Party Advertising

The embedded third-party players displayed on our Website may include advertisements served by independent ad networks. These ad networks may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing activity.

MovoraStar has no control over the advertisements displayed within third-party embedded players and does not share any user data with these ad networks.

7. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect and analyze anonymous usage data. These services use cookies and similar technologies to help us understand how visitors interact with our Website. The data collected is anonymized and used exclusively for internal improvement purposes.

8. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Any anonymous, aggregated analytics data is retained only as long as necessary for analytical purposes and is not associated with any identifiable individual.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about any data we may hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete any data associated with you.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics and cookie tracking through your browser settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@movorastar.live. Since we collect minimal data, fulfilling such requests is typically straightforward.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

11. International Users

Our Website is accessible from various countries worldwide. By using our Website, you acknowledge that your information may be processed in countries where our servers or third-party service providers are located. We take reasonable steps to ensure your privacy is protected regardless of jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the Website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Website after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
