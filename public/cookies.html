1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies are, how MovoraStar ("we," "us," or "our") uses them on our Website (movorastar.live), and your choices regarding their use.

By using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies and similar technologies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to website owners. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device for a set period) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

3. How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

MovoraStar uses cookies and browser local storage for the following purposes:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential / Functional Store user preferences such as language selection, streaming source preference, and auto-play toggle settings. These are necessary for the Website to function as intended. Persistent (until cleared)
Legal Compliance Store your acknowledgment of our disclaimer to prevent repeated display of the legal notice modal. Persistent (until cleared)
Analytics Collect anonymized data about how visitors interact with the Website, including pages visited, time spent, and browser information. We use this data to improve our service. Session / Persistent (third-party controlled)
Third-Party Embedded Content Embedded video players may set their own cookies for playback functionality, advertising, and analytics. These are outside our control. Varies (third-party controlled)

4. Local Storage vs. Cookies

In addition to cookies, we use the browser's LocalStorage API to store user preferences. Unlike cookies, LocalStorage data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
  • Does not expire automatically (persists until you clear it manually)
  • Has a larger storage capacity than cookies
  • Cannot be accessed by third parties through our implementation

LocalStorage is used for storing your language preference, disclaimer acceptance status, preferred streaming source, and auto-play toggle state.

5. Third-Party Cookies

When you access embedded content from third-party streaming providers through our Website, those third parties may place their own cookies on your device. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy and cookies policies.

MovoraStar does not control the cookies set by third-party services and is not responsible for their use. We recommend reviewing the cookies policies of any third-party service accessed through our Website.

6. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect and analyze anonymous information about how visitors use our Website. These analytics providers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data such as:

  • Pages visited and the time spent on each page
  • The website you came from (referring URL)
  • Your browser type, device type, and operating system
  • General geographic location (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. It is used solely to help us understand usage patterns and improve our Website.

7. Your Choices and Control

You have several options to control cookies and local storage:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Enable "Do Not Track" signals

Please refer to your browser's help documentation for specific instructions.

Clearing Local Storage

You can clear LocalStorage data through your browser's developer tools or privacy settings. Note that clearing this data may reset your preferences (language, disclaimer acceptance, etc.).

Opting Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Managing Cookies by Browser

For more information about managing cookies for your specific browser, visit the following resources:

9. Do Not Track Signals

Your browser may allow you to transmit a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal to websites you visit. Our Website does not currently respond to DNT signals due to the lack of a uniform standard for interpreting such signals. However, we do not engage in cross-site tracking of your browsing activity.
